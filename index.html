What is Kalestia?

Kalestia is a free-form language made by curious people. Kalestia is based on suggestions by people like you, who'd like to learn and create a new language.

  • Learn More

What makes Kalestia special?

  • An entirely new writing system

    Our community has developed a whole unique writing system for Kalestia. But there is no pressure in using it, you can use Kalestia however you'd like. You can find it here

  • Free, Open-Source materials

    Every page, every letter of Kalestia writing materials are free to use for everyone. All of them can be found here.

  • A helpful and thriving community

    There's no shame in asking someone for help. And we are more than willing to help you with every quesition you might have. You can find every way to contact us here.
