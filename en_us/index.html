What is Kalestia?

Kalestia is a free-form language made by curious people. Kalestia is based on suggestions by people like you, who'd like to learn and create a new language.

  • Learn More

What makes Kalestia special?

  • No genders for any words

    Kalestia is constructed on a completely gender neutral basis which makes it easy to learn without having to memorize genders of every word (like German, Russian, Romanic languages etc).

  • A helpful and thriving community

    There's no shame in asking someone for help. And we are more than willing to help you with every quesition you might have. If you'd like to join our Telegram community, click here.
