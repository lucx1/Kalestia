Lemia 1 - Ilya! Kue ase olia tilya?

In this unit you are going to learn the very basics of Kalestia. It teaches you how to greet, introduce yourself and ask others about their name.

Lemia 7 - Sumiasia os weliain.

It's time to go more deeply into the system of cases. Many of the English prepositions can be replaced by a simple suffix in Kalestia. Here, you will learn how to say sentences like "This is easy to me" without the need of a word like "to".

Note:
Since the units on this page have a focus on English speakers, it is possible that understanding this unit is easier to you if you already speak a language with cases (like German, Russian etc).
